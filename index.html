Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2â€“3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well mixed. Add water or milk for fluffiness.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the eggs: Once the butter is bubbling, pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to coat evenly.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the edges set but the center is slightly runny, add your fillings to one half.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette over the fillings, cook for another minute, and slide onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot with extra seasoning if desired.
